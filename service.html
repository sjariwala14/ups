Technical Specifications: 3 kVA UPS

Typical Uses of a 3 kVA UPS
  • Small to Medium Server Rooms
  • Medical Equipment
  • Telecom Equipment
  • Industrial Control Systems
  • Point-of-Sale (POS) Systems
  • Workstations & High-End Desktops
  • Small Lab Setups or Test Benches
Key Specifications
Parameter Value
Output Power 3000 VA (~2400 W usable)
Backup Time 5 to 30 minutes (depending on load)
Topology Online double conversion
Voltage Regulation Stable output during sags/surges
Potential Failure Points & Mitigation Strategies

Causes: Aging batteries, poor ventilation, heat, lack of maintenance
Impact: UPS won't provide backup during outage
Fix: Regular testing, replacement every 3-5 years, environmental monitoring

Causes: Connecting too many devices beyond rated capacity
Impact: UPS may shut down or bypass battery protection
Fix: Monitor load levels; never exceed 80-90% of rated output

Causes: Dust buildup, poor airflow, high ambient temperature
Impact: Thermal shutdown or reduced UPS lifespan
Fix: Clean vents, keep in cool room, follow installation clearances
Technology Upgrade Recommendations
Battery Upgrade

Switch from Lead-Acid to 48V LiFePO₄ Battery Pack

  • Cycle Life: >2,000-3,000 cycles (vs 500-700)
  • Charge Efficiency: 95-98% (vs 70-80%)
  • Weight: ~45-55 kg total (vs 200+ kg)
Inverter Upgrade

High-Frequency Transformerless Design with DSP Control

  • Efficiency: 92-95%+ (vs 85-88%)
  • Compact and lightweight design
  • Advanced protection features
